Why Engineering is One of the Best Majors in Our World Today

  • It sets you up for professional success

    Your entire thought process changes when you study engineering. You acquire logical thinking and critical analysis skills. Decision-making skills are improved. You become more objective and less emotional when it comes to your work. All these skills are greatly needed in the professional world.

  • Engineers will always be needed throughout time

    Engineering has been used ever since the wheel was invented. It was used to create the pyramids and by the romans to create an early plumbing system. This line of work will continue to be useful with the new and improving technology that will come.
